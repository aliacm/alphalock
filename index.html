Did you know?

Don't wait till you lose your last key before you get a duplicate! Most car keys today are transponder keys (chip keys), and the technology made its debut around the mid-1990's, but became popular in the early 2000's. Although the blade can be copied anywhere (only for a spare door key — it will not start your car), the key must be programmed through either the dealer or a professional locksmith who has the programming equipment.
Save time and money by replacing or getting additional keys made at Alpha Lock & Key!

About Alpha Lock & Key


After two years of intense apprenticeship under the tutelage of another local locksmith, we began our locksmith company in January, 1990, while pregnant with our first child. (That was risky business!) We continued as a mobile locksmith service until we opened our retail lock and key shop in May, 2000, in an established neighborhood in North Central Austin. We remain in our same location to this day, serving the Austin area and surrounding communities. We have experience in all phases of locksmithing, security, and access control hardware applications. We are still family owned and operated.
